Installation Guide

Installing your mural is straightforward with our easy-to-follow process:

  1. Surface Preparation: Clean the wall surface with a mild detergent and allow to dry completely.
  2. Positioning: Use the included grid lines to position your mural accurately.
  3. Application: Remove the backing paper gradually while smoothing the mural onto the wall.
  4. Finishing: Use the provided smoothing tool to eliminate air bubbles.

Professional Installation Available: Contact us for professional installation services in your area.
